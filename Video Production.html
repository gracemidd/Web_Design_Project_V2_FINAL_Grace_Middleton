For this assignment I was required to develop a pre production presention for an advertising film for Apple. Originally I wanted to do a ad for the brand Aesop however it was going to be too technically challenging for the limited amount of time I had. I then decided to do a video for Apple because I like using their products and thought it would be fun to make an ad for them. I decided that I wanted the video have be exciting and have a fun and happy direction because that's what it feels like when you use Appple products. The video also needed to have a tagline which was Energise with Apple. I decided that the video needed to have lots of action cut shots and inspiring music.
