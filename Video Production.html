For this assignment I was required to develop a pre production presention for an advertising film for Apple. Subject: Video Production
Assignment Number: 2
Trimester: 2
Year: 2022
Software Used: Adobe Premier Pro

For this assignment I was required to create two ten-fifteen second trailers for Apple. The videos needed to act as promotional material for the Assigment 3 production.
The first video contained a girl waking up in bed and turning her alarm off on her iPhone. She then goes into the bathroom and uses Siri to set a timer for brushing her teeth. She then sits down at her desk and gets ready for online class. At the end of the video the Apple logo is shown as well as the hashtag #EaseWithApple and link to the Apple website. The concept for the video was to portray what a typical day in the life looks like of someone who uses Apple products.
