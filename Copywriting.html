Subject: Copywriting
Assignment Number: 1
Trimester: 1
Year: 2022
Software Used: Adobe Photoshop

For this assignment I was required to make a single page ad for a brand I selected which was Bali Body. I choose them because I used to wear their fake tan all the time.
I firstly did some research about the company and what types of products they offer. I then needed to find out the demographics for the brand and come up with a psychographic to match. Once I did that I needed to research who their competitors were and do a review of the Bali Body social media channels. I then put together the ad using information I had gathered. I created the ad at home using a Bali Body product I already had. I wanted the image to have bright and fun colours to catch the attention of the target market. The message that I wanted to create was that if you use this tan you will feel confident and that will then transfer into feeling good and having a positive mindset.

For this assignment I was required to create a visual presentation for a current Australian multi-platform campaign and analysis each campaign medium. The brand I choose was Dyson because I wanted to do some research on their products. I firstly did some research about what beauty products Dyson offer and decided to focus on the Airwrap styler. I then came up with demographics and psychographics for the Dyson hair products. Once I had done that I found the Dyson ads I wanted and started analysing them. The ads were in a magazine, on their Youtube channel and on their Instagram. I then had to find competitors for Dyson which were BaByliss and Remington and then analyse ads on their social media channels.
