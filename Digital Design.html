For this assignment I was required to create a movie or tv show poster using Adobe Photoshop.I choose the tv show Euphoria because it was being aired in the same time frame as the class. I then choose a background colour gradient going from blue to purple in an upwards direction. I then looked on Unsplash for a picture of a teenage girl because that is who narrates the show and who's perspective we see. Subject: Digital Design
Assignment Number: 3
Trimester: 1
Year: 2022
Software Used: Adobe Illustrator

For this assignment I was required to select and research a crowd funding business and write a mission statement for the brand. The brand I selected was a perfume company called Eauso Vert who were on a mission to bring clean luxury to the fragrance category. Once I selected my brand I decided on the their tone of voice and some keywords to describe them. I then wrote a misson statement which was "To create unique and honest, zero waste fragrances for the new generation." After that I decided on demographics and psychographics for the brand. I then researched competitors to Eauso Vert which included Masion Margiela and Byredo. I then came up with a moodboard which mostly compromised of green gardens and spaces. After all of this research had been done I then started sketching some concept ideas for the logo. I orginally included a perfume bottle in the logo but then realised that the logo was going onto a perfume bottle and that it would look wrong. I choose to create a dodecahedron because it was the shape that most closely resembled Earth. I also traced countries using vectors to add some identifable features to the globe so that the audience knew that what was they were looking at. I then adjusted the logo to the minimum size it could be and added clear space around it.
