Subject: Digital Design
Assignment Number: 1
Trimester: 1
Year: 2022
Software Used: Adobe Photoshop

For this assignment I was required to create a movie or tv show poster using Adobe Photoshop.I choose the tv show Euphoria because it was being aired in the same time frame as the class. I then choose a background colour gradient going from blue to purple in an upwards direction. I then looked on Unsplash for a picture of a teenage girl because that is who narrates the show and who's perspective we see. After I found one I imported it into Photoshop and added liquify and Motion Blur. I also added some text to give the audience some context for what my design was portraying.
Subject: Digital Design
Assignment Number: 2 part A
Trimester: 1
Year: 2022
Software Used: Adobe Illustrator

For this assignment I was required to create a series of social media tiles to promote a song using Adobe Illustrator. I chose Sunflower by Post Malone because it has emotional lyrics and refernces different colours. The first thing I did was select which lyrics I was going to use. Once I'd done that the next thing I did was match fonts I'd chosen with specific lyrics. After that I selected different coloured background's depending on if the lyrics were happy or sad. I then applied different effects to the text
including tilting, bolding, fliping and rounding until I was happy with my designs.
