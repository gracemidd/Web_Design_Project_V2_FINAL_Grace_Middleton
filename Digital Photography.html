Subject: Digital Photography

Trimester: 2
Year: 2022
Software Used: Nikon D5500 and Adobe Photoshop

For this assignment I was required to photograph a bowl of fruit using a professional camera in different lighting conditions by using studio lights and then edit the images using Adobe software.
I choose a bowl of fruit because everyone in the class was photographing each other and so I wanted to do something different. I firsly took my photograph using a Nikon D5500 which was positioned on a left angle. I then had a light facing straight on and another light at a right angle. I then imported the photo into Photoshop and found a cloud background to mask the studio background. Once I'd done that I found a photo of the moon and of a plant and masked two different sized fruits with those images. I also added some Colour Balance and Exposure effects to take the yellow out of the photo.
