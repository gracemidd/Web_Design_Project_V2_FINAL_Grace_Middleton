Subject: Digital Photography
Assignment Number: 1
Trimester: 2
Year: 2022
Software Used: Nikon D5500 and Adobe Photoshop

For this assignment I was required to photograph a bowl of fruit using a professional camera in different lighting conditions by using studio lights and then edit the images
using Adobe software. I choose a bowl of fruit because everyone in the class was photographing each other and so I wanted to do something different. I firsly took my photograph using a Nikon D5500 which was positioned on a left angle. I then had a light facing straight on and another light at a right angle. I then imported the photo into Photoshop and found a cloud background to mask the studio background. Once I'd done that I found a photo of the moon and of a plant and masked two different sized fruits with those images. I also added some Colour Balance and Exposure effects to take the yellow out of the photo.

Assignment Number: 3
Trimester: 2
Year: 2022
Software Used: Nikon D550 and Adobe Photoshop

For this assignment I was required to capture a photograph that explored visual narrative. I choose to take a photo of an AFL game because their can be many different stories and moments that happen in a game. I used a Nikon D5500 to take the photo and then made some adjustments to it in Photoshop. These included cropping, changing the hue and making Jack Riewolt brighter. The photo is of Jack Riewolt who is sitting on the ground and reflecting after the game. This is because the game was a draw and he kicked 5 points meaning that if one of those shots had been a goal his team would have won.
